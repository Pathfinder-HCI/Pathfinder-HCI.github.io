In-Class Heuristic Eval

Brief

This mobile app helps you get outside and explore new interesting parts of your area by suggesting paths for you to walk.
You’re going to help us out by trying to perform a few tasks for us using the interface.
This is a prototype, so please be as critical as possible so we can make improvements.
It was made in Unity and currently does not have all of the map/location tracking features, and acts mostly as a demonstration.
Remember, we’re testing the interface - not you.
If you run into problems, it’s not your fault, it means we need to change something.
Your cursor acts as your finger and you can click to “tap” or “swipe”.
Some sections will continue without input to simulate walking on a path.

Scenario Tasks

  1. Leave a rating on a point of interest
  2. Customize a path by adding a point along the way
  3. Go on a 20-minute walk
